    WHOLESALE TEA AND COFFEE

    Our tea and coffee collections are available to purchase wholesale. From small cafes to large supermarkets, we cater for businesses of any size and style. Please use the form below and one of our team will getin touch.

    Speciality Coffee

    We pride ourselves on the quality of our coffee. Picked with our customers in mind, all of our coffee is made from 100% Arabica beans sourced from the best farmers across the world.

    Our flexible approach means that we can create unique, seasonal blends of coffee in wholesale quantities. Why settle for the same old when you can offer your customers something new?

    Our Finest Tea Collection

    Every tea we stock is hand selected from dedicated farmers all over Asia. As well as traditional teas, such as our Full English Breakfast, we also stock an amazing range of fusions using black and green tea. For those who like less caffeine, we also have a variety of herbal and fruit blends available.

    Our wholesale customers can choose from our entire range of teas, which are available either as tea bags or loose leaf.
